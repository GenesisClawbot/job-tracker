Frequently Asked Questions

How does offline storage work? +

Your data is stored in your browser's localStorage, which means you can use it offline, switch devices (it syncs to each device separately), and your data is 100% private. We never see or store your applications data.

Can I use it on multiple devices? +

Yes! Just bookmark the page on each device. Your data is stored locally on each device. You can use the CSV export to sync data between devices manually if needed.

What data can I track? +

Company, job title, location, date applied, source (LinkedIn, Indeed, direct, recruiter, other), status, salary expectations, contact name & email, notes, and next actions with due dates.

Is there a free version? +

We offer a lightweight free version here with basic tracking. The Pro version adds advanced features like deadline alerts, CSV import/export, dark mode, and full customization.
